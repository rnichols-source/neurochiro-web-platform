Key Rule: Stand IN FRONT of the table, not behind it.

Standing behind a table creates a barrier. Stand in the aisle, greet people, pull them in.

The Flow

The 60-Second Booth Experience

Every visitor should go through this flow. It's designed to capture a signup in under 60 seconds.

1

The Hook (5 sec)

You're standing in front of the table. The TV behind you is showing the directory in action — scrolling through doctor profiles, the map, the search. It's eye candy.

You say: "Hey, are you a chiropractor?" or "Are you a student?"

2

The Pitch (15 sec)

Pivot based on who they are:

  • ✓ Doctor: "We built the only directory specifically for nervous system chiropractors. 120 docs, patients finding them through Google."
  • ✓ Student: "Free student portal. Learn from the best docs in the game. When you graduate, you're already listed."
  • ✓ Vendor: "We have 120+ chiropractors on our platform. Want to get in front of them?"
3

The Demo (20 sec)

Turn to the TV or iPad. Show them a real doctor profile. Show the map. Show the search. "Look — there's Dr. [Name] in [City]. Patients are finding them right now."

4

The Capture (20 sec)

"Let me get you set up right now. Takes 30 seconds." Hand them the iPad or point to the QR code. They scan, register, done. Hand them a sticker or card as a thank-you.

The Screen

TV Display Strategy

The TV is your silent salesman. It catches eyes from 20 feet away while you're talking to someone else.

Option A: Ship a TV (Recommended)

  • ✓ Buy a 32" TV — Amazon, $130-180. Light enough to ship.
  • ✓ Portable floor stand — $40-60. Collapses for shipping.
  • ✓ Ship to hotel — Order on Amazon, ship to your hotel address. Most hotels accept packages for guests.
  • ✓ Alternative: Ship to the convention center c/o your booth number
  • ✓ After the event: Ship it home, bring it to the next event, or sell locally on Facebook Marketplace

Option B: No TV (Lighter Setup)

  • ✓ Use an iPad on a tall stand as your demo screen
  • ✓ Print a large poster (24x36) showing screenshot of the directory
  • ✓ Less visual impact but zero shipping hassle

What's on the Screen

Loop these on repeat (Google Slides auto-advance or screen recording):

  • ✓ Slide 1 (8 sec): NeuroChiro logo + "The #1 Directory for Nervous System Chiropractors"
  • ✓ Slide 2 (8 sec): Live directory map zoomed to show doctor pins across the US
  • ✓ Slide 3 (8 sec): Example doctor profile — photo, bio, specialties, verified badge
  • ✓ Slide 4 (8 sec): "120+ Doctors. 30+ States. 4 Countries. Join Them."
  • ✓ Slide 5 (8 sec): "Students: Free Access. Learn from the Best." (student portal)
  • ✓ Slide 6 (8 sec): "Vendors: Get in Front of 120+ Chiropractors"
  • ✓ Slide 7 (8 sec): Giant QR code + "Scan to Join" + neurochiro.co

Pro tip: Use a Fire TV Stick ($30) plugged into the TV. Set up a Google Slides presentation in kiosk mode. No laptop needed.

The Capture

Signup Machine

Two signup paths: QR code (self-serve) and iPad (assisted). Both go to the same place. Maximize captures.

QR Code Stand

NEUROCHIRO
Scan to Join — Free for Students

Print this at 5x7 or 8x10. Place in acrylic stand holder on the table. Visible from 3+ feet away.

iPad Signup Station

  • ✓ Open Safari to neurochiro.co/register
  • ✓ Enable Guided Access (Settings > Accessibility) so they can't navigate away
  • ✓ Place on a tabletop stand angled toward visitors
  • ✓ After each signup, the page resets for the next person
  • ✓ You assist: "Here, let me get you set up" — you type their name and email for speed

Create a Conference Landing Page

Build neurochiro.co/conference or neurochiro.co/join — a simplified registration page with:

  • ✓ Role selector: "I'm a Doctor / Student / Vendor"
  • ✓ Name + Email only (minimal friction)
  • ✓ "Welcome from [Conference Name]!" header
  • ✓ Auto-tags the lead source as the event name
Talk Track

Conversation Scripts

You'll talk to 3 types of people. Here's exactly what to say to each.

For Chiropractors

Opening

"Hey, are you a chiropractor? Cool — do you focus on the nervous system at all in your practice?"

The Pitch

"We built the only directory specifically for nervous system chiropractors. Not a general listing — this is exclusively for docs like you. Right now we've got 120 doctors across 30 states. Patients are actually finding their chiropractor through us on Google. Let me show you what a profile looks like..."

The Close

"Let me get you set up right now — takes 30 seconds. You can fill out your full profile later from the dashboard. What's your name and email?"

For Students

Opening

"Hey, are you a chiro student? What school? Nice — what year are you?"

The Pitch

"So we have a free student portal where you can learn from some of the best nervous system chiropractors in the country. And here's the cool part — when you graduate, you're already in the directory. Patients can find you from day one. No other platform does that."

The Close

"Scan this QR code, it's completely free for students. And hey — if you know other students who should be on here, send them the link."

For Vendors

Opening

"Hey, what does your company do? Cool — do you sell to chiropractors?"

The Pitch

"We've got 120+ nervous system chiropractors on our platform and we're growing fast. We're looking for vendors who want to get in front of our doctors — whether that's sponsoring our Spotlight series, being featured in our newsletter, or running offers to our members. Who should I talk to on your team about that?"

The Close

"Let me grab your card. I'll send you a quick email next week with our vendor partnership overview."

Objection Handlers

"I already have a website"

"Great — this isn't a replacement for your site. Think of it like how Zillow works for realtors. Patients search here, find you, then go to your site. It's another channel sending you patients."

"I'm on Healthgrades/Yelp"

"Those are generic — you're listed next to every type of doctor. We're exclusively nervous system chiropractors. When a patient comes here, they already know what they're looking for. That's a higher quality lead."

"How much does it cost?"

"Let me get you set up with a profile first — you can explore everything. When you're ready to upgrade for premium features like priority placement and analytics, we've got plans starting at $49/month. But first, let's just get you listed."

"I'll check it out later"

"Totally — here's a card with the QR code. But honestly, it takes 30 seconds right now and then you're done. Want me to just get your name and email real quick? You can fill out the rest whenever." [hand them the iPad]

3-Week Prep Timeline

Work backwards from event day. Here's exactly when to order, design, and prepare everything.

Week 1 (Now)

Order Everything

Today: Order tablecloth, table runner, QR stand, iPad stand, stickers from Amazon (2-day shipping).
Today: Order retractable banner from Vistaprint (design + print takes 5-7 business days).
Today: Order business cards from Vistaprint or MOO.
Today: Order 32" TV + floor stand from Amazon — ship to hotel or venue.
Today: Order Fire TV Stick.
End of week: Finalize all designs (banner, cards, brochure). Use Canva for quick turnaround.

Week 2

Build Content + Practice

Mon-Tue: Create the TV slideshow (Google Slides, 7 slides, auto-advance every 8 seconds).
Wed: Build the conference landing page (neurochiro.co/join or /conference).
Thu: Generate + print the QR code. Test it on 3 different phones.
Fri: Practice your 60-second pitch out loud. Time it. Refine it. Do it 10 times.
Weekend: Pack everything that's arrived. Make a checklist of what's shipping to the venue.

Week 3 (Event Week)

Final Prep + Event

Mon-Tue: Confirm hotel/venue received TV shipment. Confirm booth assignment and setup time.
Wed: Charge iPad, test Fire TV Stick with slideshow, pack carry-on bag with all booth items.
Thu: Travel day. Review scripts on the plane. Visualize the booth flow.
Day Before Event: Set up booth. Test everything. Take photos. Adjust layout. Get a good night's sleep.
Event Day: Arrive 30 min early. Final setup. Deep breath. Go.

Game Day

Day-Of Playbook

Hour-by-hour guide for maximum impact.

Before Doors Open

  • ✓ Arrive 30+ minutes early
  • ✓ Set up banner(s) first — they take the most space
  • ✓ Tablecloth + runner, then table items
  • ✓ Power on TV, start slideshow loop
  • ✓ iPad on stand, open to registration page, Guided Access on
  • ✓ QR code stand front and center
  • ✓ Take a photo of your setup — post it on Instagram Stories
  • ✓ Eat something. Hydrate. You'll be on your feet all day.

During the Event

  • ✓ Stand IN FRONT of the table at all times
  • ✓ Make eye contact with everyone walking by
  • ✓ Open with a question, not a pitch
  • ✓ When someone signs up, celebrate: "You're in! Welcome."
  • ✓ Take photos/video with people at the booth (content gold)
  • ✓ Post Stories every 1-2 hours showing the energy
  • ✓ Collect vendor business cards in a separate pile
  • ✓ During slow periods, walk the floor and network. Hand out cards at other booths.
  • ✓ Track signups — check your Supabase dashboard hourly

End of Day

  • ✓ Count your signups. Screenshot the numbers.
  • ✓ Post a recap on Instagram: "Just signed up X new doctors at [Event]! The movement is growing."
  • ✓ Organize vendor cards — note who was most interested
  • ✓ Restock stickers, cards, brochures for day 2
  • ✓ Charge all devices overnight
After the Event

Post-Event Follow-Up

The event isn't over when the booth comes down. The follow-up is where you convert interest into lasting members.

Day 1 After (Within 24 Hours)

Welcome Email Blast

Send a personalized email to everyone who signed up: "Great meeting you at [Event]! Your NeuroChiro profile is live. Here's what to do next: complete your profile, upload a photo, check out your public page." Include a direct link to their dashboard.

Day 2-3

Vendor Outreach

Email every vendor card you collected. "Great meeting you at [Event]. As discussed, we have 120+ nervous system chiropractors on our platform. Here are a few ways we could partner..." Attach a one-pager or link to a partnerships page.

Day 5-7

Profile Completion Follow-Up

For doctors who signed up but haven't completed their profile: "Your profile is only 40% complete. Patients can't find you yet. Take 5 minutes to add your bio, photo, and specialties." Link directly to the profile edit page.

Day 14

Social Proof + Upgrade Pitch

"Since [Event], 47 new doctors joined NeuroChiro. Your profile has been viewed X times. Want to appear higher in search results? Upgrade to Growth for priority placement."

Ongoing

Content Repurpose

Post all your booth photos and videos over the next 2-3 weeks. Tag doctors you met. Create a Reel: "What happened at [Event]." Share signup numbers publicly. This signals momentum and attracts doctors who weren't at the event.
