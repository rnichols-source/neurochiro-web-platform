Phase 5 — Months 12-18

Build the Moat

Make NeuroChiro so deeply integrated into how doctors practice and patients find care that switching is unthinkable.

⭐

Patient Reviews

Verified patient reviews on every profile. This is the single biggest trust driver. Doctors with 5+ reviews get a "Patient Favorite" badge. Reviews create stickiness — doctors won't leave a platform where their reputation lives.

📅

Booking Integration

"Book Now" button on every profile. Integrate with Calendly, Jane App, or build native booking. When patients book through NeuroChiro, it becomes indispensable. Revenue opportunity: booking fees or premium tier feature.

🏆

NeuroChiro Verified Certification

A professional certification that means something. Doctors apply, submit credentials, get verified. They display the badge in their offices, on their websites, on social. Every badge is a free billboard for NeuroChiro. ($199-499/year)

📊

Data & Insights Product

"NeuroChiro Insights" — quarterly market reports. Patient search trends by region. Most searched conditions. Sell to conferences, coaching programs, supplement companies. This positions you as THE authority.

📚

Continuing Education Platform

Offer CE credits through the platform. Partner with educators and technique experts. Doctors need CE hours — if they get them through NeuroChiro, they'll never leave. Massive recurring revenue potential.

📡

Patient Communication Suite

HIPAA-compliant messaging between patients and doctors. Appointment reminders. Follow-up automations. Turns NeuroChiro from a directory into the operating system for a chiropractic practice.
